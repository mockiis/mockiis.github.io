Works

Moon Cheese

Moon Cheese is a simple arcade game, it was meant to be more of a test if i could create a game that could be put on XBLIG and Windows Phone, and see what it takes to pass all verifications and test on the way to those markets.

Worked great as a learning tool while it ended a bit on light side for the game part.

WPM XBLIG

Light Zone

Light Zone was originally a local multiplayer game, but that have changed quite a bit and now instead is more like an action packed 2D space-sim rougelike.

A quite different game from what it was once before, that's still in the very early phases of development so things could change even more.

Shipquest / Mazequest

Original intended as a procedurally generated quest based game, as part of a thesis but we got sidetracked, so we started this project all over.

And made Mazequest in its wake, with turned out great for our testing purposes but not so much as an independent game, will be uploaded once we area done with it.

Sons of Eye Championship

Local Multiplayer game collection, where as the name implies is a championship of different types of local multiplayer games.

A game made specially for those rare occasion where you have a couple of friends over and a pair of extra controllers, add a little bit of beer and voila! the day is set.

still in development but give it a try and tell us what you think about it.

Try it here

Nukeplomacy

An online multiplayer only game of diplomacy and blowing other people up.
Inspired by things like Wargames and Defcon with a mixture of some boardgames mechanics.

Would you resort to conflict and use your nukes or try to be diplomatic and stay out of trouble? or would you just nuke it all?

Find out what you answer will be later this year.

Who is Mocke?

Or more formally know as Joaquin, Mocke is a nice and easy sounding nickname that's easy to pronounce and remember unlike my real name.
Currently I'm on the last touches of my final thesis, and working on some own projects like the ones above, so I'm always busy.

I'm foremost a game-programmer, but I'm quite versatile to do other parts as needed like graphics and to some degree music and sound.
Somewhat becoming a jack of all trades more often than not, the good part is that there's always something for me to do, the bad part is there's always something for me to do.

Well that'll a short introduction, if you want to know more drop me a message.
